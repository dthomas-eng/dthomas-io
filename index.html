Micro-prism alignment system

Tektronix, 2017

Designed and implemented a system that aligns and attaches a micro-scale optical prism to the end of a bundle of fiber optic cables.

Successful completion of this 6-month project led to 20% reduction in scrap for a 10+ MM/year military avionics product. The project was completed one month ahead of schedule, using 97% of the originally agreed-upon budget.

Airbus STC Analysis

Altech Aerospace, 2019

I was tapped to author a 300-page analysis validating a major alteration to the recently released Airbus A330-900neo. Client time constraints required delivery within 2 months from the project start date. A typical timeframe for similar analyses is around 6 months.

I combined my knowledge of traditional aircraft structural analysis with software engineering skills to automate the most time-intensive steps of report generation to deliver a thorough analyis of the structure ahead of the deadline.

The report was accepted by the FAA with only one round of minor edits, indicating a complete and well-organized analysis. Altech went on to win additional contracts with a major client due to my on-time delivery.
